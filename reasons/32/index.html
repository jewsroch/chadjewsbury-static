Kate has been studying Korean in earnest for about a year now and it’s been really fun to listen to her practice. I often will sit in the other room and listen to her mumble her alphabet or new vocabulary. It’s amazing to hear someone progress with something they’re practicing. So often a new skill is all internalized and can’t be noticed by outsiders, but it’s easy to hear the improvement when someone is speaking out loud every day.

Korean is very hard, and I’m continually impressed by Kate’s perseverance and by the progress that she’s made so far. Every time I hear a bit of her conversations with her teacher(all in Korean) or catch her interacting with someone in her hostel, I’m so proud and impressed.
