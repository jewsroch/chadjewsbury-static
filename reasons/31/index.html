Kate makes great smoothies. I know it’s an odd thing to love about her, but they’re great. Who knew that spinach + peanut butter + banana + almond milk + coconut water would be so good? I haven’t had a smoothie since she left for Korea and I kinda miss them. Maybe I’ll make one this weekend, but it won’t be the same. :(

I’ve been avoiding sugar and “white” foods lately as an experiment. Smoothies probably won’t fit with that plan. Fortunately, I have a cheat day every week and I’m going to make Kate make me smoothies all day long. Along with waffle, and french toast, and ham & cheese sandwiches, and pasta, and mashed potatoes.
